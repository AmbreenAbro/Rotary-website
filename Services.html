Our Services

Making a Difference Through Service

At Rotary Club Hyderabad, we believe that every small act of kindness can create a big impact. Our services are focused on helping people live healthier, safer, and better lives. We work on local community projects as well as global causes, always living by our motto — “Service Above Self.”

Health & Medical Services

We organize free medical camps, health awareness drives, and blood donation campaigns to serve those in need.

  • Free eye check-up camps
  • Polio awareness and vaccination campaigns
  • Blood donation drives

Education & Youth Development

We support schools, students, and young professionals through scholarships, counseling, and skill development programs.

  • Scholarship programs for deserving students
  • School bag and stationery distributions
  • Career and leadership training for youth

Environment Protection

We believe in protecting the environment for a better future through tree plantation, clean-ups, and green living promotion.

  • One Person, One Tree Campaign
  • Park and community clean-up drives
  • Environmental awareness seminars

Disaster Relief & Response

In emergencies, we provide quick help to affected families, delivering food packs, clean water, and medical aid.

  • Food and ration distribution during floods
  • Relief camps during heatwaves
  • Emergency health support
