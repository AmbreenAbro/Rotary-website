Mr. Wali Muhammad Jatoi

Mr. Jatoi's significant contributions to education and technology are globally recognized. He has served PAF Degree College Faisal, Karachi, SZABIST Karachi, Pakistan Embassy School in Tehran, as Assistant Director IT for the Ministry of Science & Technology, and as an IT Consultant for the Tony Blair Faith Foundation & Global Nomads Group USA. He has also served as Pakistan's Ambassador to the UN Youth Assembly. Currently, he is the National Expert on Information Technology and Allied Sciences for NAVTTC Pakistan.

Key Rotary Roles:

  • Club President (2025-2026)
  • Club Young Leaders Contact (2024-2025)
  • Club Public Image Chair (2021-2022)
  • Club Foundation Chair (2018-2019)
