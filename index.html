About Us

Rotary Club Hyderabad GALAXY is a proud part of Rotary International, a global network of professionals and leaders working together to bring positive change in society. We were founded with a goal to uplift lives and support community growth in Hyderabad, District 3271, Sindh, Pakistan.

Our members come from different backgrounds but share one mission — “Service Above Self”. We are a non-political, non-religious service organization, actively working in the areas of health, education, environment, and social welfare.

Our Services

💙

Community Welfare

We help families in need through food distribution, clean water projects, and basic living support.

🩺

Healthcare Services

We arrange free medical camps, blood donation drives, and health awareness events for underserved communities.

🎓

Education Support

We provide scholarships, distribute school supplies, and conduct career counseling for students.

🌳

Environmental Action

Our club organizes tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and promotes environmental care.

🛡️

Disaster Relief

In emergencies, we quickly respond by providing food, medicines, and essentials to affected families.

🚀

Youth Empowerment

We invest in youth leadership, professional development, and skills training for a brighter future.
